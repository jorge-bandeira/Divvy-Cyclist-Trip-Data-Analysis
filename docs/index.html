Cyclist Case Study

This case study was completed as a capstone project for the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate course.

In this project we are put in the shoes of a data analyst tasked with helping a bike sharing company named Cyclist use data to grow their buisness.

The company has two types of users, casual and annual members. Since most of the revenue comes from annual members, Cyclist aims to increase the percentage of subscribers by converting casual riders.

The specific question the data analyst is challenged with is:

How do annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic bikes differently?
